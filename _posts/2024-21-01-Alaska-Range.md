Several friends and I did an expedition to the Alaska Range to climb the Moonflower Buttress of Mount Hunter. This is an El Capitan size face covered in snow and ice with a 2,000 foot ridge above it. 
We stayed at base camp on the Kahiltna Glacier for about 5 weeks and endured bad weather with occasional sunny days. On a few of those days we were able to attempt our main objective of climbing the Moonflower.
We were unsuccesful in our attempts but successful in our side quests to climb the mini Moonflower, Mt. Francis and a high point day trip on Denali.
