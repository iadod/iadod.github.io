Cerro Torre, in Argentine Patagonia is more difficult on its "easiest" route than any other hard mountain's easy route. It requires technical climbing on rock, ice and rime in addition to a long approach and very rare weather conditions.

**Under Canvas** — I scaled Under Canvas's Manufacturing from R&D to over 50 products. I was part of the early team that took all outsourced manufacturing and brought it in house to control cost escalation and quality consistency. Most notably was the development of our "ReCirc" pump, a custom PCB which ran an Arduino to sense temperature and turn on a heat pump.

**Bozeman Operations** — I built a general contracting company to $1M in revenue. We performed work for commercial, residential and government clients across multiple states. Most notably, we developed a novel process to spray-apply "HempCrete" in a California residence.
